Net Disruption / 2015

This is a interactive sound installation which offers the visitors a space to interact with this work by the iphone and the strings.

In the field of interactive media art, artists design how to make visitors voluntarily participate in their artwork. This sound installation utilizes playful metaphors, ‘cats’ cradle’ and musical instrument. In order for visitors to easily recognize the play and act according to the artist’s intention.

For the technical part, MAX/MSP, OpenFrameworks, Xcode, and GarageBand are used in this installation.

○ Dimension: (W) 1000 x (H) 1000 x (D) 1000 m
○ Materials: Metal frame, Iphone, Strings, Speakers
○ Showcased in FutureEverything Festival, Manchester City Hall, United Kingdom
○ In collaboration with Hee-eun Kim
