Creepy Smile / 2021

A simple experimental project for the controller box.

Sometimes, when people smile and laugh, it is hard for us to catch the real emotion behind the smile because it includes ambivalent, even more, various emotions. This fact makes the smile creepy.

The project, “Creepy Smile” encourages the audiences can experience the creepy emotion with the smile faced controller box, which has two buttons (eyes) and two slider potentiometers (mouth). While the audiences are pressing buttons and controlling the sliders, they can change the colour of the divided smiley faces, also control the laughing and crying sounds.
